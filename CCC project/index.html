How it Works

Deep dive into the mathematical models powering the visualization.

Optimal Substructure

Dynamic Programming breaks the problem into smaller overlapping subproblems. It builds a table of minimum coins needed for every value up to your target, ensuring the global minimum is always found.

Greedy Heuristics

The Greedy approach follows the local optimum at each step — picking the largest possible coin. While faster to compute, it may fail to find the optimal solution for non-canonical coin systems.
